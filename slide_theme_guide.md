# Slide Theme Guide

## Overall Theme
A **modern academic-editorial theme** with a soft, muted palette and bold, high-contrast typography.  

---

## Core Visual Identity

### 1. Background
- **Main background:** `#D9D4CD` to `#D6D1CA`

---

### 2. Typography

The theme uses a **clear contrast between a strong serif header font and a clean sans-serif body font**.

#### Title / Section / Header Font
- **Font:** Inknut Antiqua (Bold)
- Used for:
  - main titles
  - section headers
  - large emphasis text

Style:
- heavy serif
- academic / editorial feel
- strong visual hierarchy

#### Body / Supporting Text Font
- **Font:** Open Sans  
- Weights:
  - **Bold** → emphasis, labels
  - **Medium** → subtitles, annotations
  - **Regular** → body text

Used for:
- descriptions
- diagrams
- annotations
- supporting text

Style:
- clean
- highly readable
- modern contrast to serif headers

---

## Color Palette

### Primary neutrals
- **Background:** `#D9D4CD`
- **Primary text:** `#111111`
- **Secondary text:** `#5A5A5A`

### Accent colors
- **Dusty blue:** `#6F8FA6`
- **Periwinkle:** `#8E9BFF`
- **Soft yellow:** `#E8E28B`
- **Contour lines:** `rgba(255,255,255,0.45)`

---

## Layout Style

- asymmetrical but balanced
- large negative space
- editorial composition

---

## Graphic Elements

### Organic blobs
- soft shapes behind titles or numbers

### Contour lines
- thin, decorative, low contrast

### Circular images
- cropped in circles
- optional thin border

### Divider lines
- `1.5px – 2px` black horizontal rules

---

## Slide Tone

- academic
- elegant
- editorial
- natural / ocean-inspired

---

## Recommended Rules

### Do
- use **Inknut Antiqua Bold** for headers
- use **Open Sans (varied weights)** for all other text
- keep spacing generous
- use muted colors

### Avoid
- mixing additional fonts
- dense text blocks
- pure white backgrounds

---

## Suggested Theme Recipe

### Fonts
- **Headers:** Inknut Antiqua (Bold)
- **Body:** Open Sans (Regular / Medium / Bold)

### Colors
- Background: `#D9D4CD`
- Text: `#111111`
- Accent: `#6F8FA6`, `#8E9BFF`, `#E8E28B`

---

## One-Sentence Summary
A **muted editorial-academic theme using Inknut Antiqua for strong serif headers and Open Sans for clean, readable body text**.
